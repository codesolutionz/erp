ERP (Enterprise Resource Planning) Documentation

ERP is a modern and responsvie small and medium business management system.This is a web based system developed using PHP development language and Codeignitor framework. Design and developed for shops, small businesses, companies and any other types of businesses. Modules included are accounting, management, stocks, invoice,user and data analysis.

ERP Key Features

Sales Reports

ERP Application is developed for different businesses and products. You can see the sales report of everyday weekly or monthly.

Purchase Reports

ERP Application is developed for different business and products. You can see the purchase report of everyday.

Products Management

You can add , edit and delete the product and then can manage it from manage option

Supplier Management

You can add , edit and delete the supplier and you can manage it from manage section

Customer Management

You can add , edit and delete the customer and then can manage it from manage section

Product Purchase

You can see the product purchase and see its benefit and lose of the company at this moment

Invoice Management

To Purchase a product or sell a product, every business need invocie system, and it is what you will get here.

Stock Management

Here have very wonderful stock management system, you can see the current condition of your business.

Accounts Management

Every business need a account management system and that's what you are getting here too.

User Guide:

Add A Product > Purchase that product > Sale the product. This is the procedure. For Barcode Scanner Please Hit enter button after scanning the code at input field otherwise products will not come.

  1. This is Home page overview, Your Today's all Sales and Purchase Report will be shown here. You also can see Product wise Sales report in everyday.


  2. To add new user just go to Software Settings > Add User and fill up the information then click save button.



  3. To manage existing user just go to Software Settings > Manage User.



  4. First You have to add supplier in the system. To add Supplier just go to > Supplier > Add Supplier and fill the form and save.


  5. After adding supplier, just go to Manage Supplier then you can see the supplier list. You can edit or delete any supplier.

  6. To add Product just go to Product > Add Product and now write here product name , description, sell price , supplier price, Model and the Supplier from drop down waht you added before at supplier part then save.



  7. To import product just go to > Product > Import Product(CVS). Now you can import any product or file(must be CVS formate).


  8. To manage product just go to > Product > Manage Product. From there you can edit or delete any product.


  9. To add new Customer just go to New Customer and fill up the form. you also can add Credit customer as well as paid customer then save changes.



  10. To add category just go to Category > Add Category. fill up information then click save button.



  11. To manage category or edit category just go to Category > Manage Category.

  12. To add purchase product just go to > Purchase > New Purchase and here add date, details of product , Custom invoice number select the supplier or you can add supplier > Item information > submit.


  13. To manage purchase just go to Purchase > Manage Purchase

  14. To check stock just go to Stock > Stock Report.from here you can see stock report of product.

  15. Now , the invoice. To create a New invoice just go to Invoice > New Invoice and write the name of customer > item information > quantity > Rate > Save and paid. You can add multiple item at one invoice.



  16. After Creating Invoice you can see the invoice and can print it also.


  17. To manage invoice just go to Invoice > Manage Invoice.



  18. To add pos invoice just go to Invoice > Pos Invoice and fill up information then click save.




    19. Account settings. If you want to add new account settings just go to Accounts settings > Here you have income and expense,Tax, account summary, cheque manager, opening and closing report.

  19. To create new account just go to Accounts > Create New Account and fill up information select account type then click save button.


  20. To manage account just go to Accounts > Manage Accounts.


  21. To Income just go to Accounts > Income and fill up information then click save button.


  22. To Add Expense just go to Accounts > Expense and fill up information then click save button.


  23. To check Account Summary just go to Accounts > Accounts Summary


  24. To add closing just go to Accounts > Closing.


  25. To check closing report just go to Accounts > Closing Report.



  26. To check today report just go to Report > Todays Report.



  27. To check sales report just go to Report > Sales Report



  28. To check purchase report just go to Report > Purchase Report .



  29. To check sales report product wise just go to Report > Sales Report(Product Wise) .



  30. To check profit report just go to Report > Profit Report.
